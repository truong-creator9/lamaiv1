### STEP 4 — Build the Complete Mechanism

Once you have the name → build the full structure:

**Part A — Overall Mechanism (Business Level):**

```
MECHANISM NAME: [Confirmed name]

ONE-LINE DESCRIPTION:
"[Mechanism name] is the [way/system/method] that helps [who]
[achieve what] without needing [their biggest obstacle]."

Example: "Cô Ngọc's 3-Layer System is the only way for stay-at-home
moms to shape animal-design pastries in 30 days without needing
natural talent."

STEPS / PHASES:
[Step 1 — Name + What it does + Why this step is needed]
[Step 2 — ...]
[Step 3 — ...]
[Step N — ...]

WHY IT WORKS:
[Real professional reason — not marketing speak]

PROOF:
[Specific results from real customers who have used this process]

WHAT COMPETITORS DON'T HAVE:
[Which step or element competitors don't do / don't know how to do]
```

**Part B — Mechanism for Each Offer/Product:**

After completing Part A → ask:

> "How many different products/services do you have?
> List them out — I'll craft a separate mechanism for each,
> based on the overall mechanism we just built."

For each offer → create:

```
OFFER: [Product/service name]

THIS OFFER'S MECHANISM:
Name: [Specific mechanism name — can be the overall name + suffix]
Example: "3-Layer System — Foundation Module"
         "3-Layer System — Advanced Shaping Module"

Which steps from the overall mechanism are used in this offer:
→ [Steps X, Y, Z]

Specific results this offer produces:
→ [Specific, measurable]

One-line sentence to use in the offer:
→ "Using [Mechanism Name], [Offer Name] helps you [result] in [timeframe]."
```
