# 210 Power Words — Sabri Suby (Sell Like Crazy)
## Use to increase pulling power of headlines, hooks, and lead magnets

---

## How to Use in Avatar Builder

When writing the **Hook** in the Messaging Strategy, choose 1-2 Power Words that match:
- The avatar's #1 pain point
- The dominant emotion (Fear, Hope, Anger, Desire)
- The approach angle (Pain-driven / Aspiration / Enemy-framing)

**Examples:**
- Pain-driven + Fear: "Alarming", "Shocking", "Warning", "Survival"
- Aspiration + Hope: "Breakthrough", "Discover", "Unlock", "Instantly"
- Proof + Trust: "Proven", "Guaranteed", "Tested", "Authentic"

## Highest-Impact Power Words for the Vietnamese Market

When adapting to Vietnamese copy, these words have the strongest impact:
- **Miễn phí** (Free) — always effective
- **Bí quyết / Bí mật** (Secret / Insider)
- **Đột phá** (Breakthrough)
- **Chứng minh** (Proven)
- **Ngay lập tức** (Immediately / Instantly)
- **Giới hạn** (Limited)
- **Bảo đảm** (Guaranteed)
- **Khám phá** (Discover)
- **Sự thật về** (The Truth About)
- **Cảnh báo** (Alarming / Warning)
