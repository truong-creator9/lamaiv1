## Process — 4 Steps

### Step 1 — Show the Theory

Present the car rental story + explain the 4 offer types + 16 specific types from `references/offer-types.md`.

The user needs to understand what they're building before answering any questions.

### Step 2 — Collect Information

Ask 2 questions:

> **Q1:** "What are you currently selling? List everything — services, products, packages, including things you're thinking about but haven't built yet."

> **Q2:** "When someone doesn't buy your main offer — where do they usually go? Do you do anything to retain them?"

After receiving answers → categorize:
```
Currently selling with revenue: [...]
Currently selling but not working well: [...]
Thinking about but not built yet: [...]
Previously sold but stopped: [...]
```

### Step 3 — Analyze + Dig Deeper

Map each offer to exactly 1 of the 16 types → identify gaps → ask deeper questions based on those gaps.

Detailed dig-deeper questions → `references/analysis-guide.md`

**Purpose of questions:** To have enough information to suggest the right offer types — tell the user this clearly.

### Step 4 — Suggest + Confirm

Suggest each appropriate offer type with specific reasons → ask for confirmation:

> "Here's the Money Model I'm suggesting. Read through and let me know:
> 1. Is there any offer that sounds unrealistic?
> 2. Is there any offer you definitely don't want to build?
> 3. Which offer do you want to go deeper on?"
