**⚡ Read `references/style-guide.md` first.**

## Writing Rules

**Short sentences, short paragraphs:**
Each sentence under 20 words. Each paragraph 1-3 sentences. Plenty of white space — readable on mobile.

**Grade-5 language:**
No marketing jargon. No English terms when a Vietnamese equivalent exists.
Instead of "optimize conversion" → use the Vietnamese equivalent.
Instead of "nurture sequence" → use the Vietnamese equivalent.

**Real proof only:**
Never fabricate numbers. Never invent quotes. Where proof is missing → mark clearly with a placeholder.

**No "guarantees":**
Use "expected outcomes" instead of "guaranteed results".

**Explain to the user:**
After each important section → add 1-2 italicized sentences explaining why it was written that way.
Example: *"This section intentionally creates pain — because people act to escape pain more than to gain pleasure."*

---

## Proof Checklist — Ask Before Writing the Sales Page

> "To make the sales page truly convincing, I need real proof.
> Do you have any of the following?
>
> □ Real customer testimonials — name + photo + specific results
> □ Real numbers — how many people, success rate, revenue
> □ Real result photos/videos
> □ Your real story — before and after
> □ The name of the person behind the offer
>
> Missing any → I can still write, I'll just use clear placeholders."
